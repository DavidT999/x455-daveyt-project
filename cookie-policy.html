At Plushie Planet, we use cookies and similar technologies to keep our website soft, stable, and easy to browse. Some cookies help core features work properly, such as remembering your navigation choices, keeping products in your cart during a session, and supporting secure account and checkout experiences.

We may also use performance cookies to understand how visitors move through the site, which pages are the most helpful, and whether parts of the experience need smoothing out. These analytics tools collect broad usage patterns rather than personal little secrets, and they help us improve the layout, loading behavior, and product discovery journey.

Functional cookies can remember preferences such as accessibility settings, reduced-motion choices, or other display options that make the site easier to use. These cookies help the experience feel more consistent when you return.

Marketing cookies may be used when enabled so we can share relevant promotions, featured plush launches, or convention announcements. When required, you can choose whether to allow those cookies. Your browser may also let you block or clear cookies, though some parts of the site may become less snuggly to use if key cookies are disabled.

By continuing to use Plushie Planet, you acknowledge that cookies may be placed as described in this policy, subject to any consent choices available to you. We may update this policy from time to time, so please check back if you want the latest version.

If you have questions about this policy or want help managing your cookie preferences, please contact our team through the Contact page and we will gladly help.
