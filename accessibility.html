Accessibility at Plushie Planet ⌃
Plushie Planet is designed to feel gentle and easy to use. We aim for clear headings, keyboard-friendly navigation, visible focus states, descriptive alt text, and readable layouts that stay usable as visitors zoom in. Product pages include meaningful labels on images and controls so shoppers can browse with assistive technologies and understand what each plush, button, and navigation link is meant to do.
Need help or want to report an issue? ⌄
We are always trying to make the site softer on the eyes and simpler to explore. If you run into a barrier, contact our team and let us know the page, device, and feature involved. We welcome feedback about captions, contrast, keyboard access, motion preferences, and screen-reader clarity, and we will use that feedback to keep improving the experience.
